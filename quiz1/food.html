Delicious Food in Pekanbaru

Bolu Kemojo

Bolu Kemojo is a traditional cake from Riau, Indonesia, known for its soft texture and rich flavor. Made from a blend of flour, eggs, coconut milk, and pandan leaves, it has a distinct green color and a fragrant aroma. The cake is often served during special occasions and celebrations, symbolizing Riau's culinary heritage.

Asam Pedas Ikan Patin

Asam Pedas Ikan Patin is a traditional Indonesian dish from Riau, featuring tender patin fish cooked in a spicy and sour curry. The dish combines the tanginess of tamarind with the heat of chili, creating a bold and flavorful sauce. Rich in spices and coconut milk, it's a popular comfort food, especially in the Malay and Minangkabau regions.

Es Laksamana Mengamuk

Es Laksamana Mengamuk is a refreshing traditional dessert drink from Riau, Indonesia. It features a unique combination of sliced kuweni mango (a tropical fruit), coconut milk, and sweet syrup. The drink is typically served cold with ice, offering a perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors. Its intriguing name, meaning "The Admiral Goes Amok," is linked to local folklore.
